Place these files in this folder before running the audio mix script:

- sea_noise.wav
- construction_rumble.wav
- welding_texture.wav

Recommended characteristics:
- sea_noise.wav: clean coastal ambience, low wind, no voices
- construction_rumble.wav: distant machinery / site rumble, no sharp transients
- welding_texture.wav: light metallic buzz/spark texture, subtle, not harsh

Target format:
- WAV
- 48kHz
- stereo
- 20s to 60s minimum
