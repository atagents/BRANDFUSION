# ✉️ Outreach Agent: Cold Email Templates

*These templates exist for the Outreach Agent to use when drafting emails in `hybrid_workflow_C`. They are designed to be extremely short, high-converting, and casual.*

## Template 1: The "We made this" Direct Approach 
*(Best for: Mid-sized E-commerce, Shopify Brands)*

**Subject:** 1M view format for [Brand Name]
**Body:**
> Hey [First Name],
>
> My team just built out a new viral short-form video template (the one that's been doing 1M+ views in the [Niche] space lately). 
>
> We rendered a version using [Brand Name]'s product to see how it looks, and the hook is crazy good. 
>
> Want me to send over the MP4 for you to check out? No pressure to buy it.
>
> Best,
> [Your Name]
> BrandFusion Agency

---

## Template 2: The Trend-Jacker 
*(Best for: Fast-moving consumer goods, Fitness, Tech)*

**Subject:** crazy idea for [Brand Name] TikTok
**Body:**
> Hey [First Name],
>
> You've probably seen that "[Describe Trend - e.g., POV: I'm a 24-year-old making 100k]" format blowing up on TikTok this week.
>
> We just reverse-engineered the hook and built an AI-rendered ad using [Brand Name] that perfectly matches the pacing. It's meant to look like high-end UGC.
>
> Do you handle ad creative, or should I reach out to someone else on the team to send the video file to?
>
> Cheers,
> [Your Name]

---

## Template 3: The "Accidental" Viral Pitch 
*(Best for: Established/Luxury Brands)*

**Subject:** quick question about [Brand Name] digital assets
**Body:**
> Hi [First Name],
>
> We're a creative studio playing around with some new high-end visual AI tools (Sora/ShotVerse), and we ended up using [Brand Name]'s aesthetic as our test subject for a new cinematic Reel format.
>
> The result honestly turned out too good to just keep on our hard drive. 
>
> Mind if I shoot over a private Drive link so your team can take a look? If you love it, we sell the full commercial rights for these.
>
> Thanks,
> [Your Name]

---

## 🛑 Agent Rules for Using Templates (System Prompt Injection)
1. **Never attach the video file directly** (it triggers spam filters). Always ask for permission to send the link.
2. **Never send automatically.** The n8n workflow must save to `pending_approvals.csv`. Human clicks send.
3. **Always personalize the first sentence** based on the specific brand generated by the ComfyUI pipeline.
