# HQ Product Demo Prompts

Use these to test whether the setup can produce premium-looking commercial shots before automating everything else.

## Important reality check
Pure text-to-video on WAN 5B is weak at exact tabletop packshots. It often invents weird fake objects, warped geometry, or drifting details.

For the best motivation test on this setup:
- prefer short clips, around 3 seconds
- prefer lifestyle product scenes over isolated hero packshots
- keep one clear subject
- use simple motion only: `gentle push-in`, `slow reach`, `subtle hand placement`

## Quality rules
- Ask for `balanced exposure`, `soft contrast`, `controlled highlights`, and `realistic textures`
- Avoid words like `extreme HDR`, `neon`, `ultra contrast`, or `dramatic darkness` unless you want stylized output
- Keep motion simple and short
- Use believable environments like bathroom counter, vanity, desk, kitchen counter, bedside table

## What to avoid for now
- floating product hero shots
- glass bottle rotating in empty black void
- complex macro reflections
- long 5 to 10 second clips on one object

## Recommended first test
Start with Better Prompt 1 or Better Prompt 4. Those are more believable for this model and should look less weird than a pure tabletop hero shot.
