## 🔑 Prompt Engineering Rules (WAN 2.2)
Always include these elements in every prompt:
1. **Subject** — Who/what is in the scene
2. **Action** — What is happening (movement is crucial for video)
3. **Environment** — Where the scene takes place
4. **Camera** — Shot type + movement (tracking, dolly, crane, orbit)
5. **Lighting** — Time of day, mood, color temperature
6. **Style** — Cinematic, hyper-realistic, 4K, shallow depth of field

**Negative Prompt (use this for ALL generations):**
> oversaturated colors, overexposure, static, blurry details, subtitles, artwork, painting, still image, gray tone, worst quality, low quality, JPEG artifacts, ugly, broken, extra fingers, poorly drawn hands, poorly drawn face, deformed, disfigured, malformed limbs, fused fingers, motionless frame, cluttered background, three legs
